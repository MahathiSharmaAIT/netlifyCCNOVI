Paint Services

Premium Auto Paint Services

From minor paint touch-ups to complete vehicle repainting, our expert technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and high-quality paints to restore your car’s original finish. We specialize in color matching using digital tools to ensure seamless results that blend perfectly with your factory finish.

Whether your paint is faded, scratched, or you're simply looking for a color upgrade, Classic Collisions is your trusted source for lasting, showroom-quality results.

Our Paint Services Include:

Precision Color Matching

We use advanced PPG Envirobase® High Performance waterborne paints with digital color matching for flawless and eco-friendly results.

Full-Body Repainting

Our full-body resprays use PPG water-based paint systems, ensuring superior durability, vibrant finish, and a reduced environmental footprint.

Scratch & Chip Repair

Minor imperfections like scratches and chips are professionally refinished using factory-grade PPG touch-up techniques for long-lasting protection.
