Collision Repair

Why Choose Our Collision Repair Services?

At Classic Collisions, we specialize in restoring your vehicle to its pre-accident condition with precision and care. Whether you've experienced a minor fender bender or a major collision, our I-CAR certified technicians use advanced tools, OEM parts, and expert techniques to get your car back on the road safely and quickly.

We work directly with insurance companies to simplify your claims process and provide real-time updates on your repair status. Our shop guarantees customer satisfaction with a lifetime warranty on body repairs.

Our Services Include:

Frame Straightening

Using computerized measuring systems for accurate realignment.

Auto Body Works

Comprehensive body repairs including dent removal and panel replacement for a flawless finish.

Glass Replacement

Precision installation of OEM-grade glass to restore safety, visibility, and factory standards.
