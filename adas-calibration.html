ADAS Calibration

Advanced Driver Assistance System Calibration

Our ADAS calibration services ensure your safety systems function with pinpoint accuracy after a collision or windshield replacement. We calibrate a range of technologies including adaptive cruise control, blind spot monitoring, lane departure warning, and parking assist systems.

Using manufacturer-approved tools and procedures, our technicians restore your vehicle's systems to OEM standards, ensuring maximum safety and performance.

We Calibrate:

Adaptive Cruise Control

Ensures safe vehicle spacing through automatic speed regulation.

Lane Departure Systems

Keeps your car safely within lane markings on the road.

Blind Spot Sensors

Detects vehicles outside your peripheral vision for safer lane changes.

Parking Sensors

Provides audible and visual cues to avoid obstacles when parking.
